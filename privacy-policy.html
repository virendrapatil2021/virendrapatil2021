1. Introduction

Welcome to MathMagic Kids — a math learning application designed to help children understand mathematical formulas and concepts through an engaging, chapter-based format.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application. Please read this policy carefully.

By downloading and using MathMagic Kids, you agree to the practices described in this document. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the application.
📊

2. Information We Collect

MathMagic Kids is designed with children's privacy in mind. We limit data collection to what is strictly necessary to operate the application and display advertising.

2.1 Collected Automatically

  • Device identifiers (advertising ID, Android device ID)
  • Device type, operating system version, and app version
  • IP address and general geographic location (country/region)
  • App usage data such as pages visited and time spent
  • Crash reports and performance diagnostics

2.2 Collected by Google AdMob

  • Advertising ID (Google Advertising ID / GAID)
  • Coarse location data
  • Device and network information
  • Ad interaction data (impressions, clicks)
AdMob's data collection is governed by Google's Privacy Policy at policies.google.com/privacy.
⚙️

3. How We Use Information

We use the information collected for the following purposes:

  • To operate, maintain, and improve the MathMagic Kids application
  • To display advertisements through Google AdMob
  • To monitor app performance and fix technical issues
  • To comply with legal obligations
We do not use your information to build personal profiles, and we do not sell your personal information to third parties.

5. Children's Privacy

MathMagic Kids is an educational app intended for children under parental supervision. We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and applicable international laws.

We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children under 13 without verifiable parental consent. The app does not include:

  • Account registration or login for children
  • Chat, messaging, or social features
  • In-app purchases by children
  • Behavioural profiling of child users
Parents or guardians who believe their child has provided personal information without consent should contact us immediately at kinadobe2022@gmail.com so we can delete such information.

AdMob ads in this app are configured to comply with Google's Families Policy and applicable regulations regarding advertising to children.
